Unicorn Apocalypse Settings

Welcome! This extension will fill your screen with unicorns and rainbows every time you click the webpage or play a MIDI note. Prank your friends, or use it as a little pick-me-up to fancy up those boring webpages at work.

The unicorns don't appear until you click/play a note several times on a webpage. This makes it more of a surprise for whoever you're pranking!

Note: To trigger with a MIDI instrument requires you to enable MIDI support in Chrome. Click here for instructions.

Don't have a MIDI instrument? Trigger the unicorns every time you click!
